Freight operates on blind trust.
That's costing you.

The Solution

One passport for every load.
Verified. Tracked. Immutable.

Freight Passport creates a single source of truth that follows your load from tender to delivery.

Core Feature

Load Passport Identity

Every load gets a unique, immutable passport ID. One canonical record that all parties reference — broker, carrier, driver, shipper. No more conflicting spreadsheets, lost emails, or "he said, she said."

Driver Verification

Assigned driver must verify via phone + location before pickup. Know exactly who has your load — verified, not assumed.

Fraud Detection

Real-time fraud scoring using 9+ rule categories. Get alerts before problems happen, not after.

Duplicate Detection

Search if a load has been booked elsewhere. Catch double brokering before your freight moves.

Document Hub

All BOLs, PODs, and receipts in one place. Driver uploads from phone, everyone sees instantly. No more hunting.

Complete Audit Trail

Every status change, assignment, and document upload logged with timestamp and location. Disputes become easy.
